Peanut Butter and Jelly

Ingredients

  • A decent, pre-sliced loaf of bread
  • Jelly, preferably grape or strawberry
  • Crunchy or extra crunch peanut butter

Directions

  1. Separate bread and lay it face down (this is important) on a flat surface.
  2. Open the vessels containing the peanut butter and fruit spreads.
  3. Using a knife, scoop out the peanut butter and jelly FROM ONE SIDE. This preserves the smoothness of the top of both spreads.
  4. Inform any within earshot that you have done this with a purpose, and that any who undo your work will suffer.
  5. Brandish your knife menacingly.

  6. I guess you should clean up your kitchen and apologize to your friends and family.
  7. Just eat the sandwich. It doesn't take a rocket scientist to figure this out.
