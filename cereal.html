A Bowl of Delicious Ass Cereal

Ingredients

  • cereal
  • a bowl
  • a cow

Assembly

  1. Go out to the store, market, bazaar, or dealer and obtain some Delicious Ass brand cereal.
  2. Come home, or in the aisle if you can't wait, pour the cereal into the bowl.
  3. Finally, get the cow and shoot fresh milk directly from the udders into the bowl.
  4. Eat it with your bare hands. Or stick your face directly into the bowl and just inhale.
